Read Watch Rate

This name was selected since it's a page to rate and review books and movies, so it represents reading a book, watching a movie, and rating it in the site. It's also short and catchy so people can remember it easily.

Purpose

This site provides a way to rate and review books and movies, it also provides the top ratings and recommendations from other users and creates a community of book and movie enjoyers.

Scenarios

What movie should I watch? / What book should I read?

Where can I rate and review the movie I just watched/book I just read?

What movies/books are popular right now?

Color Schema

Primary color: Dark purple #442242

This color will be used for the logo and any important icons that give identity to the site.

Font color: Cornsilk #FFF6DE

This color will be used for the font on the dark background and other accents.

Accent color: Orchid Pink #E7B9C1

This color will be used in details that don't require a lot of focus.

Background color: Raisin Black #1F1E20

This will be te background color for most of the site. On light backgrounds this will be the font color.

Typography

News Cycle

This font will be used for headings and in most of the rest of the site.

Newsreader

This font will be used for the reviews themselves. This is a newspaper type font so the reviews will look like an article and be easy to read. Example:

I actually liked this film, I think the story was good and entertaining, the pacing was good, there was a lot of action that I didn't expect but it was very enjoyable. It was also sad, I felt a real empathy for Taka, but I guess life isn't always fair and that doesn't mean you should betray your brother.
